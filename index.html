About

I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate in Management Science and Engineering at Tsinghua Shenzhen International Graduate School, Tsinghua University, under the supervision of Prof. Mingyao Qi. I am also fortunate to be co-supervised by Prof. Sheng Liu at the University of Toronto.

From January to July 2026, I was a visiting Ph.D. student at the Department of Industrial Systems Engineering and Management (ISEM) and the Institute of Operations Research and Analytics (IORA), National University of Singapore, hosted by Prof. Xiaobo Li.

My research interests lie in operations research and its applications to transportation, logistics, and service systems. I develop optimization models and algorithms to support the design and operation of these systems, including decision-making under uncertainty.

I expect to graduate in June 2027 and am seeking tenure-track faculty positions.

Research Interests

  • Applications: supply chain and logistics systems, transportation, and urban logistics operations.
  • Methodologies: optimization under uncertainty, deterministic optimization, and approximation algorithms.
